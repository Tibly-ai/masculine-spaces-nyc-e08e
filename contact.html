Before You Ask

COMMON QUESTIONS

Is the consultation free?

Yes. The initial consultation is complimentary and non-obligating — it's how we scope your project.

Do you work outside NYC?

Primarily Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. We consider select projects further out — mention your location in the form.

What's a typical timeline?

Single rooms run 4-8 weeks. Full apartments typically run 3-6 months depending on scope and sourcing.

Do you handle sourcing and install?

Fully. Design, procurement, contractor coordination, and final install are all handled in-house.
